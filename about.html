Background Information

Vision

A world where everyone has access to safe and clean water.

Mission

To deliver sustainable clean water solutions and empower communities for better health and dignity.

Objectives

  1. Provide clean water to 1 million people by 2030.
  2. Install 500+ water systems in high-need areas by 2027.
  3. Cut waterborne diseases by 60% in target communities.
  4. Train 10,000 locals in water, sanitation, and hygiene (WASH).
  5. Raise global awareness and influence water policy change.
